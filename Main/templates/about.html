About Tontu

Tontu is a sustainable fashion business in Dhaka, Bangladesh, providing ethically sourced and eco-friendly clothing. We strive to create a positive impact on the environment, while at the same time providing stylish and quality garments that are both fashionable and affordable. Our products are made with recycled polybags, reducing waste and promoting a circular economy. By using traditional weaving techniques passed down from generations, we work with local weavers to bring their skills to the forefront and provide them with a stable income. Our goal is to support these artisans and their communities while promoting a healthier environment

Garbage Collection

We offer weekly pickups, ensuring that your property stays clean and tidy. Our team of professionals is dedicated to providing you with prompt and efficient service. With our service, you will have peace of mind knowing that your garbage is being handled in an environmentally-friendly manner. We recycle as much as possible and dispose the rest in a proper way, so you can feel good about your contribution to the environment.

Recycled Textiles

We provide sustainable fashion solutions that create minimal environmental impact, while delivering high quality, stylish and ethically-sourced apparel. We work with responsible manufacturers and prioritize natural fibers, organic fabrics, and recyclable materials to reduce waste and carbon emissions. Our goal is to create fashionable, sustainable clothing that looks great, and does good for the planet.
